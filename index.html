7, Oct 2022 - Online event

Node-RED Con 2022

Node-RED Con 2022 is a digital technology conference for all Node-RED users. Lots of amazing projects, such as the latest case studies, stories that you can't usually hear, and technical news. Don't hesitate to join us if you've used Node-RED or not!

Registration for this conference is not required. You can watch the streaming from the following URLs.

Japanese Track: https://youtu.be/aw74tQNSOz4

About The Conference

The conference will be held this October as a free online event. The aim is to boost the Node-RED community by promoting interaction between users, showcase new technologies and demonstrate use-cases in different fields.
